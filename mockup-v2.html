Who We Are

Owner-managed.
Operator trusted.

Founded in 2003 by Paul Chimes and Elias Nyathi, Chimes Crane Hire brings over two decades of lifting expertise to every project. Before Chimes, the founders helped build Beacon Crane Hire into one of South Africa's most respected names — subsequently sold to a listed company in 1997.

Today, we operate from our Germiston yard in Johannesburg, serving clients across Gauteng with a modern Liebherr fleet and a team of certified operators who live by our founding values.
